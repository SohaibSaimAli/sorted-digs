In Person Organizing Services

Want to transform a space like a kitchen, closet, kid's room, or garage? Let us handle your next organizing project. Here's how it works:

We meet online for 20 minutes to get to know each other and discuss project details like timeline and budget.

I provide you with an Organizing Plan of Action based on our discussion.

We get you on the schedule. In general, a one hour onsite visit by me followed by a series of four hour organizing sessions works best.

We sort & purge, contain & label, and create a maintenance plan for you. You decide how involved you would like to be in the process.

I return in about a month to help keep you on track!
